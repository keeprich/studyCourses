About Us

Brilliant Synergies International Limited (BSI)-previously Brilliant Care Limited, is a Recruitment, Education and Training organisation providing 360-degree solutions to Small and Medium-Scale Enterprises within the Education, Health and Social Care sectors of the United Kingdom. Also, as an Approved Training Provider and Centre, our company and Trainers have memberships and affiliations to the Association of Healthcare Trainers (AoHT) UK, and the Association of First Aiders (AoFA) UK. We are a Dementia Champion and Dementia-friendly organisation too. Our ever increasing clientele organisations include Domiciliary Care companies, Nursing Homes, Care Agencies, Children Homes, Non-Governmental and Charity organisations across the United Kingdom.

MANDATORY TRAINING : COMPLIANCE, QUALITY ASSURANCE AND APPROVED TRAINING PROVIDER STATUS

We are quality assured and moderated by Trainercourses Ltd, an ISO 9001 achievement organisation, and the UK’s preferred Train the Trainer Provider, Awarding and Accreditation body to many of the largest healthcare organisations and associations which rely on its expert support, standardised compliance training materials and experience. The courses assist with compliance of the Care Certificate standards developed jointly by Skills for Care, Health Education England and Skills for Health, and in meeting with regulating/inspecting body training requirements. Brilliant Synergies International Limited (BSI) holds the vision of becoming a positive contributor to best practice and principles within the education, health and social care sectors through the delivery of UK-accredited training courses. Our Trainers have gained varied and relevant experience within the vocational education, health and social care sectors as Healthcare professionals, Assessors/Tutors, Care Managers, Recruitment Managers and Consultants to SMEs within the health and social care sectors of the United Kingdom.
