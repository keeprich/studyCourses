BSI Profile

BSI Receuitment and Training Ltd is a training provider offering a range of first aid courses, fire safety training, health and safety courses and food hygiene qualifications to businesses, organisations and individuals throughout the UK.

The company was established in 1999 by Leslie Nikoi, following his retirement after 31 years with the West Midlands Police. It has been a family run business ever since. The company's motto has always been to "provide high-quality training at affordable prices" and this is something the business continues to deliver. All First Response (First Aid) Ltd trainers are highly experienced, competent and friendly and our training courses are tailored to meet the needs of the customer. Courses can be held at one of First Response First Aid's training venues or delivered at your own premises.

For more information on the first aid courses, health and safety training and manual handling courses we offer please contact us today to find out how First Response (First Aid) Ltd can equip you with the knowledge and skills you need to carry out your tasks safely and efficiently
